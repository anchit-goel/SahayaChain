Our Journey

2022

Idea of SahayaChain was born to empower local communities through decentralized finance.

2023

Prototype launched and pilot tested in three rural regions with amazing success.

2024

Scaled operations across 10 states, onboarded 10,000+ users.

2025

Going global. Partnering with international organizations to expand SahayaChain's impact.

Our Values

Mission

To create a transparent, inclusive, and community-first ecosystem for borrowing and lending, built on trust and blockchain technology.

Vision

A world where anyone, anywhere can access fair and secure financial tools—powered by people, not institutions.

Core Roles

Our founders each bring something vital to the table—technology, legal insight, community engagement, marketing, and innovation—to make SahayaChain more than a platform: a movement.

Future Plans

Next steps include launching a mobile app, creating educational tools for borrowers, forming global NGO alliances, and tokenizing community trust scores.
